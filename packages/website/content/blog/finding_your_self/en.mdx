# Designing Local-First Workflows

Polywise is being positioned as an **agentic content system** rather than a traditional productivity app. This mock article explains the shape of that idea in broad terms.

## Core idea

Polywise turns saved material into structured memory, then makes that memory usable through search, relationships, and agent workflows.

## Why it matters

- Raw notes are easy to collect but hard to reuse
- Local-first storage keeps ownership explicit
- Better context improves downstream agent behavior
